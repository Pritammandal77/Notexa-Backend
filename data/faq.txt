What is Notexa?
Notexa is a platform where students and professionals can buy and sell handwritten or typed notes at an affordable price.

Who can upload notes?
Any student or professional can upload handwritten or typed notes as long as they are original and useful.

Are refunds available after purchasing notes?
No. Since notes are digital downloadable products, Notexa does not provide refunds after purchase.

How much can I earn by selling notes?
Sellers earn 70 percent of the note price. Since every note costs ₹29, the seller receives ₹20.3 per download.

What type of notes can be uploaded?
Users can upload handwritten notes, typed notes, PDF study materials, summaries, exam preparation guides, or subject explanations.

Is Notexa only for college students?
No. Notexa is open for school students, college students, competitive exam aspirants, and working professionals.

How do buyers receive their notes?
After payment, the notes become instantly downloadable from the user's dashboard.
