Anyone can sell notes on Notexa including students and professionals.

To sell notes on Notexa, users need to follow these steps:

Step 1: Create clear and well organized notes in handwritten or typed format.

Step 2: Convert the notes into a clean PDF using scanning or digital tools.

Step 3: Upload the PDF to the Notexa platform.

Step 4: Once the notes are approved and published, they become available for students to purchase.

Sellers earn money every time their notes are downloaded.
